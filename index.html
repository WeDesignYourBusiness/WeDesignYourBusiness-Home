Welcome to StaticNets

We are a start up web design company, looking to help create and improve the websites of local businesses on Long Island, and the 5 boroughs. We are 2 college students, attending John Jay, and St. Johns University.

ABOUT US


We try to make the perfect website for you and your business. We specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Between the two of us your website needs with can be met. Portfolio and static websites are the top two websites that we specialize in creating. Our purpose is to make sure that small businesses can get their name onto the internet for a low cost and high reward. Our goal is to provide you with the best service and support to create the best website for you and your business. We will work with you and shape your website to your liking.
