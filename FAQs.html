FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

GENERAL FAQ'S

What is the difference between a multi page website and a single page website?

A multi page website has more than one page to view (i.e a home page, contact page, gallery) whereas a single page website has everything on one page where users can just scroll up and down to find whatever they want.

How do I know if I should buy the business package or the personal package?

Well thats simple. If your website is for profit then you are in the business category. But if your site is just for your own personal ventures such as job hunting or displaying your skills and talents then you are in the personal category.
